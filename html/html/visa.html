Documents Requirement

Please prepare the following documents before applying for your visa.

  • Valid Passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport Bio-Data Page Copy
  • Recent Passport-Size Photographs
  • Proof of Financial Means (Bank Statement)
  • Travel Insurance Coverage
  • Flight & Hotel Reservation

Additional Requirements

These documents may be required depending on the applicant's profile.

  • Cover letter explaining the purpose of travel
  • Invitation letter from host or sponsor (if applicable)
  • Proof of accommodation from host country
  • Previous visa copies or travel history
  • Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from employer
